# Multiple Subaccounts

You may create several subaccounts all managed by the same Ethereum Wallet (EOA) / Derive Wallet (SCW). To do so, go to the [Subaccounts page](https://app.derive.xyz/subaccounts) via the left-hand sidebar:

<Image align="center" border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/0249f46069827e7ba16786c4f240dc4c80361c6c17f76f75184b51c7c4742855-app.derive.xyz_subaccounts_1.png" />

Here you will see your first subaccount with deposited funds. You may now click "Create Subaccount" and choose an account name and subaccount margin type:

<Image align="center" border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/63dd964b1dac5e106d143e86122fd2d427c9a37797f02118959aa116dbc0f369-app.derive.xyz_subaccounts.png" />

Once created, you can click on "Transfer" to move funds from your first subaccount to the newly created one.

<Image align="center" border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/9ea0b54f36181cb3cfb8bbf3df9bcc238c8589dfb362639d1f65cab6cbb06e57-app.derive.xyz_subaccounts_2.png" />
